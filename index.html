Unusual facts about Lake Baikal

Baikal is the cleanest and deepest lake in the world.

  • 01.

    Baikal is home to over 2,500 species of animals and plants

    Two-thirds of the local flora and fauna are unique here, including seals (the only species of seal living in fresh water) and fish such as omul.

  • 02.

    Lake Baikal — the oldest lake in the world

    Scientists claim that the lake could have been formed more than 30 million years ago. How old the lake really is, however, remains a mystery.

  • 03.

    As much as 20% of the world’s unfrozen fresh water is contained in Baikal alone

    The water in the lake is so pure that you can drink water from it without any additional treatment.

  • 04.

    366 rivers flow into Baikal, and only one — the Angara — flows out of it

    This river never freezes, because the water comes to the source from deep layers of Baikal, where it is warmer than on the surface.
